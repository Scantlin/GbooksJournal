Not So Big, Not So Bad (Reimagining Little Red Riding Hood)

January 29, 2026

Situation or Experience

We are so thankful kay Ms. Julia kase inadjust niya yung time ng performance namin sa Great Books. Ang napunta samin ay Little Red Riding Hood, maayos naman ang naging ka group ko, sina Ericka, Justin, Moises, Lee Laurence. Lahat kami ay talagang ginawa ang best para sa role play na iyon. From planning up until sa execution. Kahit na we faced so many challenges such as too many activities from other courses, props, to Sean Malaque who did not attend to that day, so we have to adjust everthing, because he will play the role of Hunter. So Justin and I decided to switch characters: Justin from Narrator to the Hunter, and me from Grandmother to Narrator. We are glad na pinuri ni Ms. Julia yung naging presentation namin Although we make a mistake, but those Mistake were not too big and not too bad. We thought she will going to criticize how and why we change role, but what we heard from her was purely a positive critique, also for all groups. That's why we are so happy. I also enjoy the performance of other groups. We are so shocked that we got perfect score in this presentation, it feels like lahat ng pagod, hirap, inis sa member kase minsan hindi nag paparticipate sa call at kung ano ano pang emosyon, lahat ng iyon e nawala HUHUHU kayaa sobrang thankful talaga kay Ms. Julia for appreciating our effort, also sa mga kagroup ko na ginawa rin yung best nila kahit wala kami talagang practice.

Emotional and Cognitive Response

I feel little nervous and mad after knowing that Sean will not be able to attend class. That time we have to think for alternative kase if we stick sa original plan, masisira yung flow, actually, due to a lack of members—especially girls—we decided to change the mother into a father. but the original story still visible. Instead na ipakita na kinakabahan kami ay we just remain calm, we are thankful sa time na ibinigay ni Ms. Julia for preparation, ginamit namin yun to modify the original plan. After the presentation we feel relief kase maayos naman na naexecute yung presentation namin. We are so happy and amuse the way Ms. Balag critique each group, because it is on point and we can really see that she is satisfied will all of the groups. We also take down notes the improvement she said, para next time mas maganda pa ang maging presentation.

SEL Insight

This situation shows social awareness and Responsible Decision making. When we carefully plan our modification to our existing flow, Actually hirap kami makaisip kung paano gagawin doon e, kase nga all goods na sana if pumasok lang si Sean. Justin as Narrator and Hunter goods na naman, kaso nakita namin sa script na magkasunod yung line ng Hunter and Narrator, so sabi ni Justin nakakalito siya if execution na, kaya napag isipan namin na maging Narrator na lang din ako, para maayos lang talaga yung flow nong kwento, at di rin malito yung mga manonood, di rin naman kase ako kasing galing ni Dave na kayang magpalit palit ng Character per line. In terms of social awareness, Justin and I decided to remove Sean to the group because he did not participate and contribute to the group. Also Sean apologize to us that he was unable to attend class.

Personal Reflection

Always have a backup plan, since not every initial plan will work—just like what happened to us. Never let pressure eat you up, because if it does, everything will be destroyed. I am so thankful that I have this kind of team, where everyone gives their best to improve our performance. I also forgave Sean when he apologized, even though we had already given him a sanction. I felt bad about being mad at him, especially since it wasn’t because of a mean team or simply not wanting to attend class—he had his own reasons. So what I needed to do was to understand him. However, to be fair to all the members who contributed, Justin and I decided to remove his name from the list. Nakakapagod kase ang dami talaga naming need asikasuhin and gawa, but with Ms reaction and comment nakakalimutan namin yung pagod, and mapapasabi na lang talaga kami na worth it lahat. This situation helped me improve as someone who takes the initiative to establish a plan and make tough decisions. It also taught me to always look for the brighter side of every bad situation.
